01

Why it matters

A link can carry more than the page you intended to share.

Many URLs contain tracking tags, advertising identifiers, referral tokens and social-sharing parameters.

These additions can be used to follow activity across websites, apps, social networks, search engines and advertising platforms. They often make links longer and reveal more than is needed to reach the destination.

Strip and Share gives you a straightforward way to remove supported tracking parameters before the link leaves your hands.

A shared link
example.com/article?utm_source=social&utm_campaign=share&ref=profile
